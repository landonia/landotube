About Landon

I write software for a living (if you couldn't guess that already). I have been a code monkey practically forever, gaining more experience everyday. I have had my ups (creating things is awesome) and my downs (but it can make you want to throw your computer out of the window at times). I still have so much to learn it's crazy, but I think that's a good thing.

I run my own consulting company, Landonia, from which I pimp myself out on various contract gigs.

So far I have worked for an online games company in Cambridge, a server monitoring tools company in Germany and a customer feedback company in London. I have also completed quite a few tours of duty consulting with Landonia Ltd. My latest gig involves me guiding a team of 5 developers to create a top notch advanced monitoring application. The company itself is in the energy industry

Personally, I think about stuff way too much, even the smallest things. I see something, hear something, discuss something and I let them go on in my mind over and over. It drives me crazy, but over time I think it has allowed me to think about things logically and get ideas for things I think should be created. I now want to start putting these things into practice and it starts with this blog. It will be a challenge, I am a tinkerer at heart and my mind moves quickly to new things. I joke that my harddrive is the vault of broken dreams.

The application was built using simplegoblog and written using Golang. Some of the blog design was borrowed from Ben Ogle.

I tap away at a keyboard all day like a monkey.
